Welcome To Your Haus!

Haus is a Smart Speaker & Home Interface Device designed to fit seamlessly into your home!

Looking for smarter ways to interact with the latest & greatest? The Haus Smart Speaker is the next object to bring into your room. Whether its in your bedroom, living room, home office, or kitchen, Haus can instantly turn any room into a connected space. Haus is voice interactable featuring a plethora of useful commands to aid in everyday life - with new commands being developed everyday!

Take Center Stage!

No unsightly cords to go tripping over. Haus is a low power LI-ION battery device with an impressive 3.5 to 7 day charge life between charges. With Lightning Charge Technology (TM), Haus only needs a 3 - 6 hour charge to meet the upcoming commands for the following week! Haus features a magnetic "Safety Eject" charge cable that makes it easy to plug-in and charge, while also mitigating damage to both user and device.
